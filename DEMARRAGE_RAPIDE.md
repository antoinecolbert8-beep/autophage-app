## 🎨 **DESIGN SYSTEM**

### **Couleurs:**
- Background: `bg-black` (#000000)
- Text principal: `text-white` (#FFFFFF)
- Text secondaire: `text-slate-400`
- Borders: `border-white/10`
- Hover: `hover:border-white/20`
- Backgrounds cards: `bg-white/[0.02]`

### **Spacing:**
- Sections: `py-20` ou `py-32`
- Cards padding: `p-6` ou `p-8`
- Gaps: `gap-6` (grilles), `gap-8` (navigation)

### **Typography:**
- Hero: `text-6xl md:text-7xl font-bold`
- H2: `text-5xl font-bold`
- H3: `text-3xl font-bold`
- Body: `text-xl text-slate-400`
- Small: `text-sm text-slate-500`
